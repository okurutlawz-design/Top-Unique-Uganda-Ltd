About Us

Leading Cleaning Solutions in Uganda

Top Unique Uganda Ltd is a fully registered Ugandan company specializing in providing professional cleaning and hygiene services to residential, commercial, industrial, and institutional clients.

Since our establishment in 2007, we have built a strong reputation for delivering reliable, high-quality, and efficient cleaning solutions. Our mission is to transform every space into a clean, healthy, and safe environment through professionalism, consistency, and high standards of service delivery.

We achieve this through a dedicated team of well-trained, qualified, skilled, and experienced staff, using modern cleaning equipment and eco-friendly products to ensure safe and effective service delivery tailored to meet the unique needs of each client.
